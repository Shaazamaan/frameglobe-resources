Training Module 01

The Grid Method Workbook

Mastering proportion and accuracy through structural breakdown.

How to Use This Workbook

1
Understand the Grid
Complex images are hard to draw. A grid breaks a complex image into small, manageable squares. Instead of drawing a "face," you just draw the abstract lines inside one square.
2
Focus on One Cell
Look at the reference image. Pick one square (e.g., top-left). Draw EXACTLY what you see in that square onto your blank grid. Do not look at the rest of the image.
3
Connect the Puzzle
Move to the next square. Ensure lines connect fluidly across the borders. When you finish all squares, you will have a perfectly proportioned drawing.
4
Print & Practice
Print the following pages. Use a pencil. Draw lightly first, then darken your lines.
