30-Day Challenge

Zero to Hero Sketch Roadmap

Consistency is the key to mastery. Tick one box every single day.

Week 1: Deconstruction & Control
Day 01
Draw 10 pages of straight parallel lines (Horizontal, Vertical, Diagonal).
Day 02
Draw 10 pages of circles. Aim for perfect closure and roundness.
Day 03
Connect the dots. Place random dots and connect them with straight lines.
Day 04
Draw continuous "S" curves. Fill 5 pages. Keep movement fluid.
Day 05
Draw 2D shapes: Squares, Triangles, Rectangles. vary sizes.
Week 2: 3D Form & Perspective
Day 06
Draw cubes in 1-point perspective. Horizon line in the center.
Day 07
Draw cylinders. Focus on the ellipses (ovals) at the top and bottom.
Day 08
Draw spheres. Add a simple shadow to show volume.
Day 09
Combine shapes. Stack a cube on a cylinder.
Day 10
Draw floating boxes. Rotate them in space.
Week 3: Observation
Day 11
Draw your coffee mug using only cylinders.
Day 12
Draw a book. It's just a modified box.
Day 13
Draw a fruit (Apple/Orange). Look for the sphere structure.
Day 14
Draw your phone. Pay attention to the corner curves.
Day 15
Draw a bottle. Combine cylinder (body) + tapered cylinder (neck).
Week 4: Synthesis & Shading
Day 16
Value Scale. Draw 5 boxes, shade from White to Black.
Day 17
Sphere Shading. Core shadow, cast shadow, highlight.
Day 18
Simple Still Life. Detailed drawing of 2 objects together.
Day 19
Drapery. Draw a cloth draped over a chair. Simple folds.
Day 20
Final Challenge. Draw your hand holding a pencil.
